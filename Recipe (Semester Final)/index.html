Mom's Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 1 stick grass-fed butter
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 egg yoke
  • 1 1/2 cups of einkorn flour
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

Pre heat oven to 350 degrees. Add the butter, sugar, vanilla, salt, and baking soda in to a mixing bowl and mix on low until everything is thoroughly mixed. Add the egg yoke and mix on low for 20 seconds. Add the flour and mix on low until flour is mixed in, then turn it up to medium speed and add the chocolate chips. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes, then let them cool for another 10 minutes. Enjoy!

Internet Cookies

  • 8 tbsp salted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions

Pre heat the oven to 350 degrees and microwave the butter for about 40 seconds. It shouldn't be hot, but it should be entirely liquid. Using a stand mixer or electric beaters, beat the butter with the sugars until creamy. Add the vanilla and the egg; beat on low speed until just incorporated â€“ 10-15 seconds or so. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until crumbles form. Use your hands to press the crumbles together into a dough. Roll into 12 large balls and bake for 10 minutes. Let cool and enjoy!

Ina Garten Cookie Recipe

  • 1/2 pound unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 extra-large eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts
  • 1 1/4 pounds semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

Pre heat oven to 350 degrees. Cream the butter and two sugars until light and fluffy. Use an electric mixer with a paddle attachment. Add the vanilla and the eggs one at a time, mix well. Sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt and add the butter, mix at low speed. Fold in the walnuts and chocolate chips. Use a 1 3/4 inch ice cream scoop and put the cookies on the tray. Dampen your hands and flatten each cookie before baking them. Bake for 15 minutes, let cool and enjoy.
